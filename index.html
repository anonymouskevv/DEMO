Legal Consequences of Sharing Someone’s Private MMS

Circulating, forwarding, uploading, storing, or distributing private video without consent is a criminal offense.

Applicable Indian Law:
  • IT Act Sections 67 & 67A: Obscene / sexually explicit content online.
  • IPC 354C: Voyeurism — up to 7 years imprisonment.
  • IPC 509: Insulting modesty.
  • IPC 292: Obscene content distribution.

Consequences include criminal charges, arrest, imprisonment, fines, civil lawsuits, device seizure, and a permanent record.
Any circulation of the video will lead to strict legal consequences under the IT Act and IPC.
